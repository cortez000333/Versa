The problem

Trillions in tokenized assets.
Zero secondary market.

Every RWA token is issued by a protocol and can only be exited through that same protocol. No independent cross-protocol secondary market. No price discovery. No liquidity.

🔒

No exit path

Holders are locked into each protocol's own redemption process. If you hold RealT tokens and want to exit, you find a buyer manually through Discord — or you wait indefinitely.

📊

No price discovery

There is no independent data showing what tokenized assets actually trade at in secondary markets. NAV is the only reference — but NAV is what the issuer says, not what the market says.

🤝

No trust infrastructure

Peer-to-peer trades between strangers have no escrow, no reputation system, no protection. Who sends first? Currently — whoever trusts the other person more.
